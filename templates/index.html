About JEC

Jhadeswar Engineering College (JEC) in Balasore, Odisha, is a relatively new institution established in 2025, while the Jhadeswar Institute of Engineering & Technology (JIET) has been operating since 2009.

JEC is committed to nurturing talent, fostering innovation, and contributing to technological advancement. With highly qualified faculty members and state-of-the-art facilities, JEC continues to shape future engineers and leaders.
