About JEC Balasore

Jhadeswar Engineering College (JEC), located in Balasore, Odisha, is dedicated to providing quality technical education and fostering innovation. The college offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs across multiple engineering disciplines.

With experienced faculty members, modern laboratories, and a focus on holistic development, JEC Balasore prepares students to excel in industry and research. The institution emphasizes discipline, knowledge, and innovation as its guiding principles.
