About JEC

Established in 1947, Jabalpur Engineering College (JEC) is one of the oldest and most prestigious engineering institutions in India. It offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across multiple engineering disciplines.

JEC is committed to nurturing talent, fostering innovation, and contributing to technological advancement. With highly qualified faculty members and state-of-the-art facilities, JEC continues to shape future engineers and leaders.
